Chamber Site Plan

Site Name

Name: Quito Community Chamber of Commerce

This name reflects the focus on the local community and supporting local businesses in Quito, Ecuador.

Purpose

  • The Quito Chamber of Commerce website offers a space where local entrepreneurs can discover resources, access development tools and find ongoing training opportunities for their personal development.
  • This website provides detailed information about community services and events organized by the Chamber of Commerce.

Scenarios

  • What are the upcoming events of the Chamber of Commerce?
  • How can I register as a member?
  • What benefits do I get by joining the Chamber?
